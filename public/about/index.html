About

Biography#

Hiroki Takeda (武田 博樹)

I am currently a first-year Master’s student (M1) at Osaka University, Japan. My academic background is in Mathematics (B.Sc.), and I am currently applying mathematical structures to Information Science.

I have a strong passion for Numerical Linear Algebra. My goal is to develop efficient algorithms for high-dimensional data analysis by bridging the gap between pure mathematics (algebraic structures) and numerical computation.

I mainly use the Julia language for my research and numerical experiments.

Personal Interests#

Track & Field (Athletics)#

I am a sprinter specializing in 100m and 200m at the Osaka University Track and Field Club. I served as the Vice Director in 2025 and will assume the role of Director in the 2026 season. I value discipline and consistent effort, both in research and sports.
