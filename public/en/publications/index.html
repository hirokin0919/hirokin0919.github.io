Research Interests#

My research interests lie in Numerical Linear Algebra, with a particular focus on the theoretical and algorithmic aspects of Tensor Decompositions.

I am specifically working on the following topics:

  • Tensor Train Decomposition
    I investigate efficient algorithms and properties of the Tensor Train (TT) format, which is a powerful tool for breaking down high-dimensional tensors into lower-order components to mitigate the curse of dimensionality.

  • Optimization on Tensors
    I am interested in solving optimization problems involving tensor structures. This includes developing numerical algorithms for finding optimal low-rank approximations and handling tensor completion problems.

  • Generalization of Matrix Structures
    A key part of my theoretical research focuses on extending fundamental matrix concepts to higher-order tensors.
